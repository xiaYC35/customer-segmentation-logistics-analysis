# customer-segmentation-logistics-analysis
Customer segmentation and logistics performance analysis to assess delivery delays impact on satisfaction and support targeted commercial actions for high-value customers.

## Objective
This project aims to analyze customer behavior in an e-commerce context by:
- Segmenting customers based on purchase behavior
- Measuring the impact of delivery delays on customer satisfaction
- Identifying premium customers (BC & TBC) affected by significant delivery delays (>10 days)
- Providing actionable business recommendations

## Key Analyses
- Customer segmentation based on order frequency and average basket value
- Chiffre d’Affaires (CA) analysis for 2017
- Delivery delay computation and distribution
- Relationship between delivery delays and customer satisfaction scores
- Identification of high-value customers eligible for compensation actions

## Business Insights
- Strong negative relationship between delivery delays and customer satisfaction
- Significant drop in ratings starting from 2–3 days of delay
- Premium customers affected by long delays represent a key retention risk

## Deliverables
- Segmented customer dataset
- List of premium customers eligible for a commercial gesture
- Visual analytics supporting decision-making

## Tools
Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
